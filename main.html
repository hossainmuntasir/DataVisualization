Life Expectancy of OECD Countries

Introduction

Welcome to our interactive data visualization page! Here, we explore life expectancy across different countries over the years. Use the year filter to see how life expectancy changes over time. Click on a country in the world map to see detailed demographic data in the bar chart. Select a year below to start!

World Map by Life Expectancy

This interactive map displays the life expectancy of countries around the world. Hover over a country to view its life expectancy for the selected year. Click on a country to see detailed demographic data in the charts on the right. Please note, interactivity will begin once a year is selected from the top!

Demographic Data by Age and Gender

The bar chart displays demographic data by age and gender for the selected country and year. It illustrates the distribution of life expectancy across various age groups within the population.

Life Expectancy Over Time

The line graph shows the life expectancy trends over time for the selected country. Use this chart to observe how life expectancy
has evolved over the years. Click on "Add Country" button to compare more countries!
